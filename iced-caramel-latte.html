Iced Caramel Latte

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes
  • Full milk
  • Coffee
  • Sugar to taste
  • Caramel syrup
  • Vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. Take an empty glass.
  2. Add 1/4 cup milk, coffee, and sugar.
  3. Mix with a frother or fork until combined.
  4. Add a handful of ice cubes.
  5. Fill the rest of the glass with milk.
  6. Add caramel syrup.
  7. Top with one scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  8. Enjoy.
