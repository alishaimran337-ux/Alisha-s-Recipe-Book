Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500 ml) cold heavy whipping cream
  • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Pour the cold heavy whipping cream into a large bowl.
  2. Beat the cream using a hand mixer or stand mixer until stiff peaks form.
  3. Pour the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract into the whipped cream.
  4. Gently fold the mixture with a spatula until smooth and fully combined.
  5. Do not overmix, or the cream may lose its fluffy texture.
  6. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container or metal loaf pan.
  7. Press a sheet of plastic wrap directly onto the surface to prevent ice crystals from forming.
  8. Freeze for at least 6 hours, or preferably overnight, until firm and scoopable.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
