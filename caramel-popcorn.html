Caramel Popcorn

Ingredients

  • 1 bag popped popcorn (about 8 cups)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup corn syrup or honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°F (120°C).
  2. Place the popped popcorn into a large heat-safe bowl.
  3. Remove any un-popped kernels.
  4. Combine the butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup in a saucepan.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat while stirring constantly.
  6. Once boiling, let it cook for exactly 4 minutes without stirring.
  7. Remove the saucepan from the heat.
  8. Quickly stir in the baking soda and vanilla extract.
  9. Allow the caramel to foam and become lighter in color.
  10. Immediately pour the hot caramel over the popcorn.
  11. Gently stir until all the popcorn is evenly coated.
  12. Spread the popcorn onto a lined baking sheet.
  13. Bake for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
  14. Allow the popcorn to cool completely.
  15. Break into pieces and serve.
