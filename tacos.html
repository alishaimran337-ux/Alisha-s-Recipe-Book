Tacos

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 8 hard taco shells or small soft tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until browned.
  2. Drain any excess grease from the skillet.
  3. Sprinkle the taco seasoning mix over the beef.
  4. Add 1/3 cup of water and stir well.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 5 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  6. Warm the taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave.
  7. Spoon the seasoned beef into each shell.
  8. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the hot meat.
  9. Add a handful of shredded lettuce.
  10. Top with your favorite salsa.
  11. Serve immediately while warm.
