Hash Browns

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and grate them using a box grater.
  2. Place the shredded potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain the potatoes completely using a colander.
  4. Squeeze the potatoes in a clean towel to remove as much moisture as possible.
  5. Heat the butter or oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Spread the shredded potatoes evenly in the hot skillet.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Press the potatoes down gently with a spatula to form a flat layer.
  9. Cook for about 5 minutes without stirring until the bottom is golden brown.
  10. Carefully flip the hash browns.
  11. Cook for another 4 to 5 minutes until the second side is crispy and golden.
  12. Remove from the pan, drain on paper towels, and serve.
